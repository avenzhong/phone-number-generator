Introduction

Welcome to Phone Number Generator ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website at phonenumbergenerator.dev.

Information We Collect

Our service is designed with privacy in mind:

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from our users.
  • No Generated Number Storage: All phone numbers generated on our website are created entirely in your browser. We do not store, log, or transmit any generated numbers to our servers.
  • No Account Required: You can use our service without creating an account or providing any personal information.

Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal tracking technologies:

  • Analytics: We may use privacy-focused analytics to understand general usage patterns. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users.

Third-Party Services

Data Security

Since we don't collect personal data, there is minimal risk to your privacy. All phone number generation happens locally in your browser using JavaScript.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy
